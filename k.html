88 Crosshill Rd

88 Crosshill Rd
Wynnewood, PA 19096

  • Price: $925,000
  • Bedrooms: 6
  • Bathrooms: 3
  • Square Feet: 3,204
  • Inquiry: 215-993-2241

This charming colonial carriage home offers the benefits of Narberth living together with the modern conveniences of a renovated house, and is located within the award-winning Keller School District. Filled with character, this is the original home on an intimate cul-de-sac neighborhood of modern French colonial houses, situated just a short walk from downtown, with it's unbeatable combination of shops, restaurants and family events, plus the region's easiest commute. The first floor has beautiful hardwood floors with a great room, dining area, powder room, and convenient laundry nook. The updated eat-in kitchen has lots of light, granite countertops and newer stainless steel appliances. A large bedroom is also located on the first floor, offering flexible living space - as a guest or main bedroom, or as additional family living or play space, complete with wall to wall carpeting, a decorative fireplace, en suite bathroom, large walk in closet and outside access. Three more bedrooms can be found on the second floor, which features great vaulted ceilings; two of the bedrooms share a Jack and Jill bathroom, and the other has its own bath en suite. The walk-out basement offers storage now and possibilities for the future. There's a private driveway and cute yard with a patio and a large deck for outdoor living. Extensively renovated in 2009, updates include newer kitchen and bathrooms, plumbing, roof, windows, gas heat and cooking, central AC, and recently painted exterior with new aluminum trim cladding to help minimize exterior maintenance. Most homes in the area of this size at this price point need significant work and updating, but not this one. This Borough beauty is great deal for the savvy buyer, and ready for you to move right in!
