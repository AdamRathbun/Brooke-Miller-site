New construction condo development in incredible location! Stately brick building with three units, outdoor space, and covered car port. Main level duplex down with spacious 3 bed, 2.1 bath floor plan. Soaring 9'4" main level ceilings, natural hardwood flooring, and design forward fixtures and finishes. Open concept living area with large windows and fireplace highlighted by floor to ceiling paneled surround. Entertainer's kitchen featuring modern flat panel cabinetry with black hardware, Thermador appliances, island with striking quarts waterfall counter and bar seating, and butler pantry.
